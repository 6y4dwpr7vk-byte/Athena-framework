Contributing

The Athena framework benefits from ongoing refinement and application across diverse contexts. Contributions are welcome in several forms:

Case Studies

Application of the framework to new institutional contexts contributes to empirical validation and methodological refinement. Contact us if you have conducted boundary analysis using the framework and wish to share findings.

Methodological Refinements

Proposals for framework extensions, classification refinements, or diagnostic improvements are welcomed. Such contributions should maintain consistency with theoretical foundations while enhancing practical applicability.

Educational Materials

Development of teaching materials, workshops, or training resources helps expand framework accessibility and application. Materials that maintain academic rigor and institutional tone are particularly valued.
